## System Summary

- **Water source:** 3/4" tap, flow rate 2400 L/h
- **Total system demand:** ~875 L/h (runs all at once, no zones needed)
- **Pipe sizing:** 25mm main, 16mm secondary
- **Drip emitters:** 1.6 L/h, 30cm spacing
- **Drip line spacing:** 30cm (vegetables), 40cm (flowers)
- **Watering schedule:** Early morning, 30–45 min daily (automated timer)
